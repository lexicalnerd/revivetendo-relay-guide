Revivetendo Relay Network — Setup Guide

Set up Revivetendo's custom Pretendo relay on your homebrewed Wii U. This enables online video calling on Wii U Chat and access to Revivetendo's Juxt communities.

Installation Steps

Follow these four steps to connect your console to the relay.

Step 1

Check Your Homebrew Setup

Your Wii U must be running the Aroma Homebrew Environment with Coldboot enabled.

Why Coldboot is Required

Coldboot starts Inkay when the console powers on so SpotPass services can connect properly. Older methods like Tiramisu or Haxchi are not supported.

Prerequisites

  • Wii U running Aroma with Coldboot enabled.
  • SD card reader or FTP connection to your console.
  • Pretendo Network ID (PNID).
Step 2

Download Inkay Switcher

Download the Inkay Switcher application to switch between server relays on your console.

Inkay Switcher (.wuhb)

Wii U Aroma Application

Download Release (.wuhb)
Download revivetendo_installer.wuhb from the Assets section on GitHub.

SD Card Placement

Copy revivetendo_installer.wuhb into the /wiiu/apps/ directory on your SD card:

Directory Path 
sd:/wiiu/apps/revivetendo_installer.wuhb

When you put the SD card back and start your Wii U, the Revivetendo Installer icon will appear on your HOME Menu.

Revivetendo Installer application tile (Rv Installer) on your Wii U HOME Menu.
Step 3

Install the Custom Relay

Configure your console to route Wii U Chat to Nico's server.

  1. Open Inkay Switcher: Launch Revivetendo Installer from your Wii U HOME Menu.
  2. Select Custom Relay: Choose Nico Christmann. (To return to standard Pretendo later, select Juxtaposition instead).
    Select "Nico Christmann" from the switcher menu.
  3. Install and Reboot: Select your environment, press A to install, then press B to reboot.
    Confirmation prompt shown before rebooting.

Verification Check

After rebooting, check the top-left banner on the HOME Menu. It should say: Using Pretendo Network (Nico Christmann).

In-Game Plugin Menu

Open the Plugin Menu anywhere on your Wii U by pressing L + + - or START. Select Inkay (Nico Christmann) to manage connection settings or reset your WaraWara Plaza.

Step 4

Verify & Get Approved on Wii U Chat

Wii U Chat requires quick manual approval to whitelist your PNID.

  1. Launch Wii U Chat: Open Wii U Chat from the HOME Menu while running the custom relay.
  2. Send Handshake: Connecting sends your PNID to the pending queue for verification.
  3. Start Calling: Once approved by an admin, your account is whitelisted and calling will work permanently.

Check Approval Status

Check your approval status or ask for verification in the Revivetendo Discord server.
